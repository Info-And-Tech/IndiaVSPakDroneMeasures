🇮🇳 India-Pakistan Drone Crisis – May 2025 🇵🇰

🚨 Overview

On May 8, 2025, Pakistan reportedly launched drones and missiles targeting Jaisalmer, India. The Indian military intercepted all threats, and there were no casualties reported. Learn more: NDTV Live Report

🔍 Conflict Background

Recent tensions escalated after the Pahalgam attack in April. India responded with air strikes, triggering retaliation. Details: Reuters Coverage

🛡️ Civilian Safety During Drone Attacks

  • Move to a secure, interior room during air raid sirens
  • Keep a 3-day emergency kit (food, water, medicine)
  • Avoid panic and listen to official announcements

More safety info: Ready.gov - Official Preparedness Guide

📵 Digital Hygiene Tips

  • Disable GPS, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi when not needed
  • Don’t livestream your location or events
  • Update apps with minimal permissions

Guide: EFF Phone Safety Module
